Hayden Wolff

Co-Founder/ Camp Director

Hayden was born and raised in Englewood Florida. He attended Lemon Bay High School for three years then transferred to Venice High School in 2018. He is the starting quarterback at Western Michigan University. Throughout the course of his college career, Hayden has appeared in 36 career games with 29 career starts. Hayden has four years of youth coaching experience, and has directed multiple camps with 50+ participants.

Weston Wolff

Co-Founder/ Tight-End coach


Weston was born and raised in Englewood Florida. He attended Venice High School and graduated in the class of 2021. He is a current starting tight-end at the University of South Florida. Throughout the course of his college career, Weston has appeared in 15 games, with 13 career starts. Weston has three years of coaching experience.

Our mission statement

At Wolff Elite, our mission is to empower young football players through elite-level training while fostering the development of character and leadership skills. We are dedicated to providing role model figures who inspire and guide our athletes to achieve their full potential both on and off the field. Committed to giving back to the community that supports us, we aim to instill values of integrity, teamwork, and resilience in the next generation of athletes.

Our goals

At Wolff Elite, our goals are as ambitious as our dreams for the youth we serve. We aim to establish the biggest camp in southwest Florida, setting the standard for excellence in football training. With a unique approach, we exclusively feature Division One football players as mentors, ensuring unparalleled expertise and inspiration for our young athletes. Through our programs, we are committed to teaching, developing, and mentoring the next generation of football stars, instilling not just skills but also values that will shape their futures both on and off the field.
