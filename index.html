Camp Itinerary

My passion and goal is to help you make your business standout.

Camp Itinerary

8:00 - 8:45 AM Check-in

Gates open. All registered campers must check in BEFORE 8:45 to receive camp T-shirt. Dynamic warmup and combine testing begin immediately after registration

9:00 - 9:30 AM Combine Testing

Each athlete will be tested in the following exercises:
- 40 yard dash
- Pro Agility Shuttle
- Broad Jump

9:45 - 10:00 AM Dynamic Warmup

Athletes will be coached through a Division 1 football warmup routine

10:05 - 10:35 AM Agility Stations

Athletes will be separated into age groups and will run through a circuit of 5 drills designed to enhance agility

10:40 - 11:10 AM Position Specific Drills

Group will break up into desired position and will be coached through the fundamentals of their position

11:10 Lunch Break

Athletes will be provided boxed lunch

11:30 - 12:00 PM Recruiting Q &A With College Athletes

Parents and athletes will have the opportunity to ask questions and gather information from our Division 1 coaching staff about college football and the recruiting process

12:00 - 12:10 PM Warmup

Athletes will go through a brief dynamic warmup and break up into their position groups

12:15 - 1:00 PM Position Specific Drills

Athletes will learn position specific skills, fundamentals, and techniques

1:05 - 1:30 1v1 Competitions

Athletes will be separated into age groups and will compete in the following 1 on 1 competitions:
- WR vs DB
- OL vs DL
- TE/RB vs LB

1:35 - 1:45 PM Fastest Man Competition

Using results from the 40 yard dash, the top 3 fastest athletes from each age group will have the opportunity to race for the title of "Fastest Man "

1:45 - 2:00 PM Awards

To conclude camp, our staff will present an award to one athlete from each age group for the following titles:
- Fastest Man
- Offense and Defense (lineman) "Big Man Award "
- Overall Offensive and Defensive MVP Award
